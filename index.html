Profile

I'm an integrated M.S.–Ph.D. student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Inha University, advised by Younggun Cho in SPARO Lab. I also received my B.S. in Electrical Engineering from Inha University. During my M.S., I worked on robust state estimation through learned inertial odometry, and I'm now studying motion generation for robust and adaptive robot behavior. For my personal thoughts and research experiences, please check out my :)
